Apex Systems Leave Policy
Effective January 1, 2026

This document defines all categories of paid and unpaid leave available to Apex Systems employees. It applies to all regular full-time employees based in the United States. Employees in other jurisdictions should consult the local addendum maintained by the HR Business Partner for their region.

Paid time off (PTO)
Full-time employees accrue PTO at the rate of 1.67 days per month, for a total of 20 days per year. Accrual begins on the first day of employment. PTO includes vacation, personal days, and discretionary time away from work. Up to five unused days may be carried into the following calendar year; the remainder is forfeited on December 31. Employees leaving the company are paid out for all accrued but unused PTO on their final paycheck, subject to state law.

Sick leave
Sick leave is provided separately from PTO at 10 days per year, front-loaded on January 1 or on the start date for new hires. Sick leave may be used for personal illness, medical appointments, or to care for an immediate family member. Unused sick leave does not carry over and is not paid out on separation. Absences of three consecutive days or more require a note from a licensed healthcare provider.

Parental leave
Apex provides 16 weeks of paid parental leave for the primary caregiver and 8 weeks of paid parental leave for the secondary caregiver. Leave may be taken in a single continuous block or split into two blocks within 12 months of the birth, adoption, or foster placement. Parental leave runs concurrently with FMLA where applicable. The returning employee has the right to the same or an equivalent position at the same compensation.

Bereavement leave
Employees receive up to 5 paid days of bereavement leave for the loss of a spouse, child, parent, sibling, or domestic partner, and up to 2 paid days for the loss of an extended family member. Additional unpaid leave may be requested through the HR Business Partner.

Jury duty and civic obligations
Apex pays full salary for employees called to jury duty or subpoenaed as a witness. The employee must submit the court summons to payroll. There is no cap on the number of jury duty days covered.

Sabbatical
After five continuous years of service, full-time employees are eligible for a four-week paid sabbatical. Sabbaticals must be scheduled at least 90 days in advance and approved by the employee's department head. A second sabbatical becomes available after ten years of service.

Company holidays
Apex observes 11 paid holidays each calendar year: New Year's Day, Martin Luther King Jr. Day, Presidents' Day, Memorial Day, Juneteenth, Independence Day, Labor Day, Thanksgiving Day, the day after Thanksgiving, Christmas Eve, and Christmas Day. Employees required to work on a company holiday receive a compensatory day off within the same quarter.

Leave request procedure
PTO and sick leave are requested through the HR portal. Requests of more than three consecutive days require manager approval at least two weeks in advance. Parental leave and sabbatical requests must be filed with HR at least 30 days before the intended start date, together with supporting documentation.

Unpaid leave
Unpaid personal leave of up to 30 days may be granted at manager discretion after all accrued paid leave has been exhausted. Benefits continue during approved unpaid leave, with the employee responsible for the standard employee premium contribution.

Questions about leave eligibility or scheduling should be directed to the HR Business Partner for the employee's department.
